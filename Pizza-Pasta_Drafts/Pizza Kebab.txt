Pizza Kebab

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 cup cheese
1 tablespoon tomato puree
1 tablespoon Anjou pear, kebab

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Methodology
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.	Make a pizza base and top with Cheese,tomato & kebab.

2.	Put it in the oven for 20 minutes then take it out and decorate with extra toppings and a stuffed crust.
