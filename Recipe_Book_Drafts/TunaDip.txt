Tuna Dip

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Ingredients
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
1 (6 ounce) can tuna in water
8 ounces cream cheese, room temp
2 -3 garlic cloves, crushed
1 dash garlic salt
1 -2 tablespoon mayonnaise

-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Methodology
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.	Mix all the ingredients together, let the flavors blend.
